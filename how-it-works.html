How Laso Patch Accelerates Healing

01

Prepare the Area

Gently clean the wound or scrape with mild soap and water. Pat the area dry with a clean cloth. Ensuring the area is dry helps the natural adhesive stick perfectly to your skin.

02

Apply the Patch

Peel the biodegradable backing off the Laso Patch. Center the herbal infusion pad over the wound and press the edges firmly onto your skin. The bamboo cellulose provides a flexible, breathable seal.

03

Let Nature Heal

Once applied, the combination of turmeric, neem, and aloe vera works immediately to reduce inflammation and prevent infection. The collagen infusion promotes faster cellular regeneration, significantly reducing scarring.

04

Compost After Use

When the wound is healed or you need to change the patch, gently peel it off. You can dispose of the Laso Patch directly in your compost bin, where it will fully biodegrade within a few weeks, leaving zero plastic waste.
