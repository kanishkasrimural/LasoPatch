Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Designed for Today

The Inspiration

Laso Patch was born from a simple observation: modern wound care is filled with plastics and synthetic chemicals, while nature has provided us with powerful healing ingredients for centuries. We looked to the traditional use of banana leaves in natural medicine and combined it with the potent antibacterial properties of turmeric, neem, and aloe vera.

Our mission is to provide a wound care solution that heals your skin rapidly without leaving a permanent footprint on our planet. Every Laso Patch is a testament to our commitment to natural healing and environmental sustainability.

Our Core Values

Sustainability First

Uncompromised Purity

Compassionate Care
